ABOUT US



PawPals is created by us - a group of animal-loving university students from Adelaide, Australia. We understand the heartbreak of losing a furry friend and the joy of bringing them home, which is why we built a safe and supportive space to reunite lost pets with their families.



Using AI-powered image matching, PawPals helps pet owners find their missing companions faster and more accurately. Our platform also connects rescuers, and the community to ensure that every lost pet gets the best chance of returning home.



Because every paw deserves a way back to the ones who love them!
